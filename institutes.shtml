Centers and Institutes

Our research centers and institutes are where new discoveries happen and inform the latest approaches to medicine, health, and patient care. From shedding light on the implications of health care policy to uncovering the basic mechanisms that fuel cancer, Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences (RBHS) centers and institutes break new ground.

School-Based Centers and Institutes

Many RBHS centers and institutes are based in individual RBHS schools and at University Behavioral Health Care.

  • College of Nursing
  • Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy
  • Graduate School of Biomedical Sciences
  • New Jersey Medical School
  • Robert Wood Johnson Medical School
  • Rutgers School of Dental Medicine
  • School of Health Related Professions
  • School of Nursing
  • School of Public Health
  • University Behavioral Health Care

Additional RBHS Centers and Institutes

Additional Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences research centers and institutes include the following:

  • Cancer Institute of New Jersey
  • Center for Advanced Biotechnology and Medicine
  • Environmental and Occupational Health Sciences Institute
  • Institute for Health, Health Care Policy, and Aging Research

Rutgers also is home to hundreds of other centers and institutes that work collaboratively across disciplines with RBHS, with other Rutgers units, and with other institutions worldwide.
