  • Student Portals

    Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences students access their student data through one of two information portals.

    • myPortal
      If you are a legacy UMDNJ student, your myUMDNJ has been renamed myPortal. You will now use myPortal to access your course enrollment and financial aid information.
    • myRutgers
      If you are a student affiliated with the Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy or College of Nursing, use myRutgers to access your course enrollment and financial aid information.

  • International Services at Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences

    Serving the specialized needs of international students, faculty, staff, and their dependents, the Office of International Services is a valuable resource. Whether you are applying for or have an F-1, J-1, H-1B, or O-1 visa, have questions on your immigration status, or want more information on life in the United States, they can help. Learn more.

  • Got a Question?

    If you have a question, connect with RU-info. Staffed by Information Assistants, highly trained, service-oriented students, the referral center helps you find the answers you need about all things Rutgers. Call 732-445-INFO (4636).
