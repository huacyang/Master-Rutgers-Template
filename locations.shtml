Locations and Directions

Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences (RBHS) has multiple locations across the state. Primary facilities are found at the Rutgers–New Brunswick Campus; at Rutgers Health Sciences at Newark, Scotch Plains, Somerset, and Stratford; and at the Rutgers–Newark Campus. With a network of hundreds of health care and educational affiliates, RBHS has a presence in every one of New Jersey's 21 counties. These partnerships enable us to touch the lives of many New Jerseyans every day.
