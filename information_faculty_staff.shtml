  • myUMDNJ Legacy Users

    If you are a faculty or staff member in a legacy UMDNJ position at Rutgers, you should note that myUMDNJ has been renamed myPortal. You will now use myPortal to access your employee data and other services that you formerly viewed through myUMDNJ.

  • International Services at Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences

    Serving the specialized needs of international students, faculty, staff, and their dependents, the Office of International Services is a valuable resource. Whether you are applying for or have an F-1, J-1, H-1B, or O-1 visa, have questions on your immigration status, or want more information on life in the United States, they can help. Learn more.

  • Get the Scoop

    The Faculty and Staff Bulletin is a great source of information on the latest happenings at Rutgers:

    • Announcements
    • Appointments & Retirements
    • Awards & Honors
    • Campus Classifieds
    • Gifts & Grants
    • In Memorium

    Be sure to share your news with the Rutgers community using online submission forms. In addition to a weekly enewsletter, the bulletin provides a website with the current news, submission guidelines, and an archive of previous issues.

  • Got a Question?

    If you have a question, connect with RU-info. Staffed by Information Assistants, highly trained, service-oriented students, the referral center helps you find the answers you need about all things Rutgers. Call 732-445-INFO (4636).
