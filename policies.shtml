Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences Policies

Effective July 1, 2013, the New Jersey Medical and Health Sciences Education Restructuring Act has provided for most schools and units of UMDNJ to be transferred to Rutgers. As a result of the ongoing process of integrating the policies and procedures, you may see changes to this website and the individual policies themselves.

All members of the Rutgers community, including students, faculty, and staff in Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences (RBHS), and faculty and staff in legacy UMDNJ positions, should read and understand the policies contained within the University Policy Library, which includes policies related to compliance, HIPAA, and risk management.

Please note: If there is a conflict between applicability as expressed in the policy, and applicability as set forth in the PDF below, applicability as expressed in the policy governs. You may direct any questions to the Office of the RBHS Chancellor.
